Lil Uzi Vert

Lil Uzi was born in 1994 and is from North Philadelpha. Before dropping out, he began rapping in High School due to his friend rapping and he wanted some of the hype. Uzi's first small hit came in 2014, called "UZI."

"Eternal Atake" (Coming Soon)

Uzi's next project "Eternal Atake" has been long awaited. This album has been surrounded with lots of drama. The cover art to the left, was heavily influenced by the symbol of the "Heaven's Gate" cult. The remaining members of the cult did not appreciate the album cover, as they spoke on the matter saying that this is copyright infringement. It's not certain whether this is still the album cover or not. Recently Uzi announced that the album was finish, but sadly, it may never release. Lil Uzi recently came out and said that he quit music, but I do not think he was serious and am still anticipating the release of Eternal Atake.
